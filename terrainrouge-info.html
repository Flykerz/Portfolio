Terrain Rouge

The Web App for Collaborative Music Playlists at Polytech Nancy

This project is a creation of a 4th-year engineering student.

From Static Playlists to Collaborative Fun

During Polytech Nancy's student association events, playlists are used to set the musical mood. But wouldn't it be even better if everyone could contribute?

Introducing Terrain Rouge, a web application designed to revolutionize event playlists! It allows for collaborative creation, ensuring everyone's musical preferences are heard.

Shape the Soundscape of Your Events

  • Open to All Polytech Nancy Students: Create an account and join the music-making fun!
  • Collaborative Playlist Creation: Suggest songs and vote for your favorites for each event.
  • Event-Specific Playlists: Karaoke nights, rock concerts, astronomy evenings - you name it, Terrain Rouge has it covered!
  • Democratized Choice: The event creator sets the deadline and maximum song limit, ensuring a diverse and voted-upon final playlist.
  • Export with Ease: Once the event is over, the finalized playlist can be exported to your preferred streaming platform!

The Name's Inspiration: The "Terrain Rouge"

The name "Terrain Rouge" (Red Floor) pays homage to the well-trodden dance floor where countless Polytech Nancy events and concerts take place. It embodies the energy and spirit of student life!

The project is currently under development, with a target launch date of September 2024 for all Polytech Nancy students. Get ready to rock your favorite events with collaboratively chosen music!
