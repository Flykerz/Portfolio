E-commerce Website Redesign

Enhancing user experience to increase conversion rates.

Project Overview

The goal of this project was to redesign an e-commerce platform to improve user experience and increase sales. The previous website had outdated design and usability issues that resulted in high bounce rates. By implementing a modern and intuitive interface, the project aimed to enhance customer satisfaction and retention.

Process and Methodology

The redesign process involved user research, wireframing, and iterative testing. We used Figma for design prototyping and React.js for front-end development. The project also included the integration of a new content management system and payment gateway. One challenge was ensuring compatibility with mobile devices, which was resolved through responsive design techniques.

Results and Impact

The redesign resulted in a 30% increase in conversion rates and a 50% decrease in bounce rates. User feedback highlighted the improved ease of navigation and visual appeal. The project also contributed to a significant boost in overall customer satisfaction and engagement, leading to a projected annual revenue increase of 20%.
