About Polytech Nancy

Polytech Nancy is a leading engineering school located in Nancy, France. It is part of the University of Lorraine and offers a wide range of engineering programs in various disciplines.

With a commitment to academic excellence and practical learning, Polytech Nancy prepares students for successful careers in the ever-evolving field of technology and engineering.

Programs Offered

Polytech Nancy provides a diverse set of engineering programs, including but not limited to:

  • Computer Science Engineering
  • Electrical Engineering
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Civil Engineering
  • Chemical Engineering

Facilities and Campus

The campus is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities, including modern laboratories, research centers, and collaborative spaces. Students have access to resources that enhance their learning experience and foster innovation.
